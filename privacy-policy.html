Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how Waraq Altoot HRMS, developed and maintained by Waraq Altoot Technologies, collects, uses, and protects user data.

How We Use the Information

  • To provide HR services such as attendance tracking, leave requests, and shift changes.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties unless legally required.

Data Security

We use appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access or disclosure.

Location Data

Our application may access and process location data to provide location-based services and improve user experience. Location information may be collected while the app is in use or running in the background, depending on the features enabled by the user.

We do not sell or share users’ location data with third parties except when required to provide core app functionality or comply with legal obligations.

Users can control or disable location permissions anytime through their device settings.
